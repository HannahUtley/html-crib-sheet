Preheader

The preheader is the first couple of lines of text you see underneath the Subject Line, when the email is received in the recipient's inbox. It won't be displayed in the body of the actual email.

Place the following code directly after the opening body tag and before any other text in the email.


<div style="font-size:0px;line-height:1px;mso-line-height-
rule:exactly;display:none;max-width:0px;max-height:0px;opacity:0;
overflow:hidden;mso-hide:all;">
<!-- Add 85-100 Characters of Preheader Text Here -->
</div>

Replace <!-- Add 85-100 Characters of Preheader Text Here --> with the preview text of your choice.

See example below:


<div style="font-size:0px;line-height:1px;mso-line-height-
rule:exactly;display:none;max-width:0px;max-height:0px;opacity:0;

Discover our range of products.
</div>
