Font Styling

Email Safe Fonts

Email-safe fonts work across different email clients.

There are only 6 email-safe fonts to choose from, so using Google Fonts can provide greater design flexibility.

List of standard email-safe fonts

  • Arial
  • Courier New
  • Georgia
  • Times New Roman
  • Trebuchet
  • Verdana

How to add a Font Family


<!-- Use font family, with your desired font first, 
and your backup fonts following. 
In the example below Arial is our desired font but 
Helvetica acts as a backup font -->

font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;

<!-- Add the above code to the style tag within the <td> 
containing your text -->

<table class="full-width mob-pad-6" width="520" align="center" 
border="0" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="0" style="width:520px">
	<tbody>
		<tr>
			<td class="cntr darkmode" style="font-family: Arial, 
			Helvetica, sans-serif;font-size: 14px;line-height:20px;
			color: #000000;">
			
			Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, 
			sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore 
			magna aliqua.
			
			</td>
		</tr>
	</tbody>
</table>
